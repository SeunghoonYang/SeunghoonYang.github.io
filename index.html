About

Research profile, education, and current thesis topic.

My doctoral research centers on lightweight semantic road marking segmentation and vector map descriptors for urban driving localization and mapping. I am also interested in LiDAR/Camera/IMU/RTK-GPS fusion, radar-camera sensor fusion, autonomous mobility platforms, and robot navigation in indoor and outdoor environments.

I received my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from KAIST, where my thesis was on radar-camera sensor fusion for vehicle pose and velocity estimation in extreme environments. I received my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from KAIST.
